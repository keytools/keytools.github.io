Description

Free online tool to easily pick a color and construct a palette of colors

The design and the animations of this website was mainly inspired from this website and we really want to thank its creators for their great job!

Once a color is selected, the color formats (HEX, RGB, HSV, ...) and color variations (Shades, Tints, Tones, ...) will be displayed accordingly and once hovered, two buttons will appear:

  •    which will let you copy the HEX code of the color to your clipboard
  •    which will let you add this color to your palette

To reorganize your palette, you can Drag & Drop the colors to change their positions. Personally we find this feature very useful and we hope you find it as well.

For new inspirations, you can generate new colors by clicking the button present on the top right corner of the selected color and on the palette section.
